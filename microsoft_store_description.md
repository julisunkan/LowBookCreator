# Low-Content Book Creator - Professional KDP Publishing Suite

## Description
Unlock the power of self-publishing with the Low-Content Book Creator, the ultimate web-based suite designed specifically for KDP (Kindle Direct Publishing) entrepreneurs. Whether you're creating journals, planners, notebooks, or coloring books, our tool streamlines your workflow from concept to print-ready PDF.

This comprehensive application offers a range of powerful features to help you scale your publishing business:

### Key Features:
*   **Custom Notebook & Planner Generation:** Create perfectly sized PDFs with customizable headers, page numbering, and multiple layout options (Lined, Dot Grid, Graph Paper).
*   **Intelligent Prompt Integration:** Enhance your journals with a database of randomized prompts across various categories like Gratitude, Self-Reflection, and more.
*   **Coloring Book Converter:** Transform your images into coloring-ready outlines with our advanced image processing engine.
*   **Bulk Generation Engine:** Scale your production by uploading a simple CSV file to generate hundreds of unique book interiors in seconds.
*   **KDP Trim Size Validation:** Ensure your books are industry-standard with built-in validation for popular KDP sizes (6x9, 8.5x11, 5x8, etc.).
*   **Automated Title Generator:** Never get stuck on a title again with our built-in creative title assistant.
*   **Browser-Based PDF Preview:** Instantly verify your interior layouts before downloading.
*   **Secure & Private:** Automatic background cleanup ensures your uploaded assets and generated files are securely deleted after one hour.

Stop wasting hours in manual design software. Start building your publishing empire today with the Low-Content Book Creator.
